Innovation and Vision between Enterprises and Research Institutes ( EN version)

in italiano, per cortesia ...

Science and Knowledge: a sound scientific basis achieved through studies in physics has been brought to the more abstract and formal logical structures (theories of grand unification, gravity, high energy physics) with the PhD and, then, enriched with several insights in other disciplines (environmental sciences, organization, egovernment, research governance) and completed by a twenty-year experience in the field of Information & Communication Technologies, ICTs - my current main specialization.

Business and Consulting: knowledge becomes a means to support enterprises in their improvement towards innovation. Process innovation is pursued by following recognised quality standards and then streghtened via the relation with main local business communities. The long-time consulting and professional teaching experience in the ICTs is an aid to support enterprises for change management and for process (sometimes also product) innovation.

Business Proposal: advice and support on Information Technology (IT) Governance, Key Performance Indicators (KPIs), SLA (service level agreements) -based management and contracting, System Quality Management (SQM) support and audit, IT service management, security and environment (ISO9000, ISO20000, ISO 27000, ISO 14000 certification schemas); audit and process monitoring (ISO 19011). Preparation, coordination and management of National, Regional, European innovation projects and management of incentives; research in process and product innovation; hints/tools on social media marketing strategies; technical and scientific publishing; training and dissemination in ICT, QMSs, IT Governance, Integrated Management Systems, innovation.
